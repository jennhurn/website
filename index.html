About

I'm drawn to the problems where
the path doesn't exist yet
and someone has to make it.

That's the job, whether it's building a go-to-market motion from scratch, designing the programs that make a team actually work well together, or figuring out how to bring a new idea into the world before anyone's done it before.

I care deeply about the people inside the work. Teams should be places where people thrive. Experiences — whether for customers, employees, or a room full of practitioners — should be thoughtful and worth someone's time. That orientation shapes everything I do, from how I run a campaign to how I design an offsite.

I'm excited about what comes next. The problems I haven't encountered yet, the teams I haven't built yet, the ideas that don't have a home yet. Whatever it is, I'll bring the same thing I always do — a belief that the best work is human-oriented, and that delight is something worth leading toward.

Selected Work

Some things I've built, launched, and made real.

  • 01 Agentic Data & Analytics Bootcamp
    ✦ AI Assisted Marketing & Sales

    5,000+ registrants across two cohorts — built from scratch, without a playbook. Curriculum, lab infrastructure, speaker logistics, partner co-marketing with Microsoft Azure. The goal was simple: give data teams a real on-ramp to agentic AI, not just another webinar to half-watch on a Friday.

  • 02 Ascend Website Redesign
    ✦ AI Assisted Brand & Marketing

    Eight weeks from "this site isn't working" to a full rebrand and 150+ page Webflow site. We rewrote the narrative, restructured everything, and partnered with FruitBowl Digital to get it live. Two weeks after launch: 5x increase in conversions, double the traffic. Also, Otto got a glow-up.

  • 03 Onboarding Program
    ✦ AI Assisted People & Ops

    Built Ascend's onboarding experience from scratch — pre-day-one communication, week-one structure, role-specific ramp paths, a buddy system, and 30/60/90 check-ins. The goal wasn't compliance. It was belonging and capability, and it worked: 96% of new hires reported feeling more effective from day one.

  • 04 Agentic DE Certification — 101, 201 & 301
    ✦ AI Assisted Education

    Three courses, 21 modules, built in 48 hours. Two years of Ascend's published work on agentic data engineering existed but wasn't connected — practitioners had to collect the pieces themselves and figure out the order. A hackathon deadline finally forced the question. What came out the other side was the ADE Learning Path, and the way it got built is its own story.

  • 05 Core Value Formation
    ✦ AI Assisted Culture

    Refreshed Ascend's peer recognition program by rethinking it around our four core values — I Got It, 1 Team 1 Dream, Spark Delight, and Consistently Curious. Then turned those values into a real in-person experience: a scavenger hunt through Cal Ave, LEGO challenges, micro-research sprints, and a quarterly awards ceremony. The values held up in real decisions. That's the bar.

  • 06 AI SDR Outreach Motion
    ✦ AI Assisted Revenue

    Getting outbound right was iteration — we tested AI SDR tools like Artisan, HubSpot's agent, and others before landing on AmpleMarket. Micro-campaigns of 20–50 people beat big blasts; the signal layer separated research from spam. Pipeline scaled without proportional headcount; the metric was conversation quality, not volume.

  • 07 Offsites, Hackathons & Team Events
    ✦ AI Assisted People

    For a remote-first team, offsites aren't a nice-to-have — they're infrastructure. Multi-day retreats, team hackathons, internal Hackdays with elaborate Avengers theming (don't ask). The work was in the details: who sits next to whom, what the food situation is, whether the activities match the team's actual energy. 50% improvement in eNPS.

  • 08 Built In Best Places to Work
    Culture

    Four consecutive years. The Built In award runs on employee survey data, which means you can't manufacture it — if your team doesn't feel it, the score reflects that. Winning four years in a row was less about the award and more about having evidence that the programs we were building were actually working.

  • 09 Field Events & Webinar Program
    ✦ AI Assisted Demand Gen

    Field events as a pipeline engine — Snowflake Summit, Databricks Summit, TDWI, booths of up to 20 people, diligent follow-up on the return. Webinars on Sequel.io: new sessions in hours instead of days, an event hub on the site, and a program that compounded into blogs, sequences, and series.

  • 10 Plans & Pricing Rollouts
    ✦ AI Assisted GTM & Strategy

    New platform, new pricing from the ground up — fair to existing customers, honest about AI-era costs, no surprises for people who'd been with us before. A 14-day developer free trial with real spreadsheet discipline. Holding product, sales, finance, CS, and customers in one coherent story until the launch landed invisibly.
