About

If there are two things I love, it's
projects and puzzles.

I'm drawn to the problems where the path doesn't exist yet and someone has to make it. That's been true whether I was building a go-to-market motion from scratch, designing the programs that make a team effective, or figuring out how to bring something into the world before anyone's done it before. The through-line is always the same: find the puzzle, figure out the pattern, make the thing real.

What makes that work meaningful to me is that it's always about people — the team doing the work, the customers receiving it, the practitioners in the room. I think a lot about human flourishing: what it takes for people to do their best work, feel genuinely connected to what they're building, and actually enjoy being part of it. That shapes everything from how I design an onboarding program to how I run a campaign.

And I love this work. The craft of it, the problem-solving of it, the moment something clicks into place that didn't exist before. I think that's why I've found such great delight in working with AI. It's a tool to make this work more efficient, effective, and delightful for others; a way to think bigger and move faster toward something worth making.

The exciting thing is, the world is always full of puzzles to solve. I'm ready to show up the same way I always have: with curiosity, commitment, and genuine delight in building something new.

Selected Work

Projects, puzzles, and other obsessions pursuits.

  • 01 Agentic Data & Analytics Bootcamp
    ✦ AI Assisted Marketing & Sales

    5,000 registrants across two cohorts. We built a two-day, multi-track practitioner bootcamp from nothing in six weeks — speakers, partners, full content architecture. The category was new and so was the playbook. By the end, people weren't just learning about agentic data engineering. They were building with agents.

  • 02 Ascend Website Redesign
    ✦ AI Assisted Brand & Marketing

    We were launching a new product and our website wasn't telling the right story. I led a full overhaul — new positioning, new design system, new content architecture — shipped in six weeks. Website traffic doubled. Conversions increased 5x. Also: Otto got a glow-up.

  • 03 Onboarding Program
    ✦ AI Assisted People & Ops

    Onboarding existed but it wasn't consistent — different roles, different levels of support, different experiences depending on who you happened to sit next to. I built a program that worked the same way regardless of role or time zone. 96% of new hires reported feeling more effective from day one.

  • 04 Agentic DE Certification — 101, 201 & 301
    ✦ AI Assisted Education

    We wanted to establish Ascend as the definitive voice in agentic data engineering. So I built a three-course certification curriculum — 101, 201, and 301 — in 48 hours. The twist: I used a multi-agent workflow to build it. Research agents, writer agents, eight parallel reviewer agents with distinct personas. The architectural patterns the curriculum teaches are the same ones that built it. Two years of exploration and work went in. Twenty-one production-ready modules came out.

  • 05 Core Value Formation
    ✦ AI Assisted Culture

    A core values program that worked fine on paper and didn't feel alive. Redesigned the recognition model, ran an in-person rollout with a scavenger hunt through California Avenue. The bar: do the values change anything? Ours did.

  • 06 AI SDR Outreach Motion
    ✦ AI Assisted Revenue

    Getting outbound right was iteration — I tested AI tools like AI SDR, Artisan, HubSpot's outbound agent, and others before landing on AmpleMarket. AmpleMarket gave us a highly customizable agentic framework where we could leverage our own agentic expertise to tune messaging and craft more effective outbound than any other tool allowed. Other learnings: Micro-campaigns of 20–50 people beat big blasts; the signal layer separated research from spam; LinkedIn outreach was far more effective than any other channel.

  • 07 Offsites, Hackathons & Team Events
    ✦ AI Assisted People

    For a remote-first team, connection isn't a nice-to-have — it's infrastructure. I orchestrate the cadence that made it real: quarterly in-person kickoffs, quarterly themed Hackdays, weekly all-hands, and "Demo Days" for the team to show off their work. Within my first six months at Ascend, we improved our employee engagement rates by 50%.

  • 08 Built In Best Places to Work
    Culture

    Building an award-winning culture requires a lot of intentionality. Winning four consecutive Built In Best Places to Work awards was less about the award and more about creating a space for everyone on the team to thrive.

  • 09 Field Events & Webinar Program
    ✦ AI Assisted Demand Gen

    For four years, I owned Ascend's events program end to end — from large industry conferences to a webinar program I built from the ground up. The work was equal parts strategy, logistics, and follow-through. The outcome was thousands of marketing qualified leads a year and a brand presence that showed up consistently wherever our customers were.

  • 10 Plans & Pricing Rollouts
    ✦ AI Assisted GTM & Strategy

    Rebuilding pricing from the ground up is one of the hardest things a company can do — there are a lot of stakeholders, a lot of competing interests, and a lot of ways it can go wrong for customers. I held product, sales, finance, CS, and existing customers in one coherent story from start to finish. The launch landed without a single surprise.
