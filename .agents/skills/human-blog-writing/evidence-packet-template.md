## Jenny / Human Source Material

Capture real human experience before writing.

Questions to ask Jenny when needed:

1. Which real situation made you want to write this?
2. What did you actually do, say, build, decide, or change?
3. What was messy, surprising, hard, funny, or not obvious at the time?
4. What artifact exists from this work: doc, spreadsheet, Slack post, deck, email, screenshot, workflow, rubric, metric, or decision?
5. What would you tell someone doing this tomorrow?

- Counterpoints that keep the post honest.
