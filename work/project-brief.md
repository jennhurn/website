# Project Brief — Jenny Hurn (hurn.co / personal site)

**Last updated:** 2026-04-15  
**Status:** Active — Phase 0–1 complete; **Phase 2** (approval + fact-check + writing) **→ Phase 3** (deploy)

---

## Purpose

A personal website that serves as a **professional home base** and **credibility signal** for a mixed professional audience (founders, executives, collaborators, peers). It should make a first-time visitor think: *sharp, high-trust operator with real range — and an actual point of view.*

The site is **homepage-first**, with architecture that can grow into dedicated writing pages later.

---

## Success Criteria

- **Clarity:** Someone understands who Jenny is and how she works within one scroll of the opening + About.
- **Differentiation:** It does not read as generic “AI operator” or resume-site copy.
- **Range:** Selected work shows breadth (GTM, people, ops) with **outcome-led proof** — including metrics and named callouts where accurate (fact-check before deploy).
- **Human signal:** Warmth and care come through without performative “personal brand” tone.
- **Contact:** Clear, low-friction paths to email and LinkedIn; no form required.
- **Constraints:** Works **without a headshot**. Deploy target: **GitHub Pages** + custom domain when ready.

---

## Audience

**Primary:** Mixed professional audience — no single persona dominates.  
**Balance:** Mostly professional; selective personal texture (voice, “Now” section).

---

## Non-Goals

- Not a LinkedIn clone, skills matrix, or testimonial wall.
- Not anchored in a past chapter (e.g. nonprofit era) as the spine of the story.
- Not “Ascend marketing site” — Ascend is context, not the headline.

---

## Positioning — Source of Truth

### Through-line

**Builder/Operator.** She figures out what needs to exist and builds it — often before a path exists. That is the constant across chapters.

### What’s unusual

Genuine **range** plus **taste and judgment**. She can do work most people with her title can’t. She **actually cares** about the work, people, and outcomes — in a non-performative way.

### POV on AI / tools

AI is powerful, but it is only as effective as the **teams and people** operating it. The leverage is building healthy teams and equipping them to use tools well

### Voice (Jenny’s words — use as north star)

> I turn ambiguous, important problems into real solutions. That's the job, whether it's building a go-to-market motion from scratch, designing the programs that make a team actually work well together, or figuring out how to bring a new idea into the world before anyone's done it before. I'm drawn to the early-stage problems — the ones where the path doesn't exist yet and someone has to make it. I care deeply about the people inside the work. Teams should be places where people thrive. Experiences — whether for customers, employees, or a room full of practitioners — should be thoughtful and worth someone's time. That orientation shapes everything I do, from how I run a campaign to how I design an offsite. I'm excited about what comes next. The problems I haven't encountered yet, the teams I haven't built yet, the ideas that don't have a home yet. Whatever it is, I'll bring the same thing I always do — a belief that the best work is human-oriented, and that delight is something worth leading toward.

### Independence from Ascend

Ascend is **context**. The story is bigger than any one company.

### Contact intent

Open: future roles, advisory, collaborations, interesting conversations — **without** hard-coding a single CTA frame.

## Site Structure (Locked)

1. Opening statement
2. About (includes POV — woven in, not a separate manifesto block)
3. Selected work (project cards, outcome-first)
4. Writing
5. Now — Reading / Thinking about / Building (one bullet each)
6. Contact

---

## Selected Work — Principles

- **Format:** Cards in three clusters (**Marketing & Sales**, **People**, **Operations**). Each card: **title** → **impact line** (metric or punchy callout; key figures wrapped in `.card-stat` for emphasis) → **short description** (longer narrative is OK in current prototype; trim if IA changes).  
- **Must-include:** Agentic Data & Analytics Bootcamp; Category creation (“Agentic Data Engineering”); Onboarding program.  
- **Additional projects** fill out range (brand, events, Built In, AmpleMarket, ops, etc.) — see `work/index.html` for current list (~13 cards).
