# Homepage Content Outline

**Purpose:** Map the six locked sections to reader outcome, tone, and rough copy direction. Final wording lives in `work/index.html`. Long Selected work copy and metrics are in place; Phase 2 = approval, fact-check, optional trim, then deploy.

---

## 1. Opening statement

**Purpose:** Land positioning before scroll. Not a tagline stack or job-title hero.

**Reader should feel:** “I know who this person is in one beat — competent, human, not performing.”

**Direction (2–3 short lines max):**

- Lead with the operator frame: ambiguous problems → real solutions.  
- One line that signals range without listing functions.  
- Optional: one line that hints at “what’s next” without sounding like a job search ad.

**Avoid:** “Builder / operator / strategist” triplets; Ascend as the first word; stats.

---

## 2. About

**Purpose:** Who Jenny is **and** how she thinks — **woven together** (no separate “POV” section).

**Reader should feel:** Judgment, care, and specificity — “she’s not recycling LinkedIn boilerplate.”

**Copy blocks (suggested flow):**

1. **The job** — turn ambiguous, important problems into real solutions; early-stage paths that don’t exist yet.
2. **Human orientation** — people inside the work; experiences worth someone’s time; teams where people thrive.
3. **AI / tools** — powerful, but effectiveness is a people-and-teams problem first.
4. **Delight** — worth leading toward (keep this — it’s distinctive).
5. **Closing beat** — excitement about what’s next; open stance.

**Tone:** Short paragraphs. Warm, direct, no throat-clearing (per `USER.md`).

---

## 3. Selected work

**Purpose:** Proof of **range** through **projects and initiatives**, not roles or timelines.

**Reader should feel:** “She’s done real things across GTM, people, and ops — and she can point to outcomes.”

**Format (shipped):** Three clusters with **~13 cards** total. Each card:

- **Title** (`h4.card-title`)  
- **Impact line** (`p.card-impact`) — metric or punchy callout; key fragments in `**span.card-stat`** (highlighted)  
- **Description** (`p.card-description`) — how she did it / context (current prototype uses **long** paragraphs; optional future: shorten or link to a case note)

**Must-include (locked):**


| Project                                        | Tag               |
| ---------------------------------------------- | ----------------- |
| Agentic Data & Analytics Bootcamp              | Marketing & Sales |
| Category creation — “Agentic Data Engineering” | Marketing & Sales |
| Onboarding program                             | People            |


**Also on page (see `index.html`):** Field events, brand refresh, webinar program, AmpleMarket; offsites/hackathons, values, Built In; internal comms, pricing rollouts, HR/compliance.

**Before deploy:** Fact-check every number and claim in Selected work.

## 4. Writing

**Purpose:** Show intellectual honesty and voice — not “coming soon” placeholders.

**Reader should feel:** “I could read more of this.”

**Direction:**

- 2–3 pieces, adapted from **Jenny’s LinkedIn posts** (paste into `work/inputs-needed.md` when ready).  
- Each card: title, 1-line hook, link to full HTML page under `work/writing-drafts/` or eventual `/writing/`.

**Until posts are provided:** Use one short published-style piece derived from the approved voice paragraph in the brief (temporary stub, replace when LinkedIn text arrives).

---

## 5. Now

**Purpose:** Living, human signal — updated ~monthly.

**Structure (shipped):** Three blocks — **Working on** / **Thinking about** / **Everything else** (short paragraphs; not strictly one bullet each).

**Tone:** Specific, not performative.

---

## 6. Contact

**Purpose:** Low-friction, high-signal connection.

**Include:** Email + LinkedIn (URLs in `work/index.html` — update when domain/email final).

**Avoid:** Forms, “book a call” widgets (unless Jenny adds later).
