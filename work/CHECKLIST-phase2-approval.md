# Phase 2 — Copy Approval Checklist

Use before deploying to GitHub Pages.

- **Homepage** — Every section approved line-by-line (`work/index.html`).
- **Selected work** — Outcomes and framing approved; **numbers and metrics** verified against source; no claims you wouldn’t defend in conversation.
- **Writing** — At least two pieces are real adaptations from LinkedIn (or intentional originals), not placeholders.
- **Inputs** — `work/inputs-needed.md` checklist complete (domain, email, posts).
- **Legal / employer** — Any Ascend-specific claims reviewed for accuracy and tone.

**Sign-off:** Date and initials in `decisions.md` when ready for deploy.
